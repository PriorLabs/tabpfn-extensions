Add a `bayesian_optimization` extension using TabPFN as the surrogate model, following the PFNs4BO approach (Mueller et al., ICML 2023): `expected_improvement` computes EI in closed form from TabPFN's predicted bar distribution (no Gaussian assumption), and `propose_next_point` screens random candidates and refines the best ones by gradient ascent on EI via `differentiable_input=True`. Ships with an end-to-end example on the Hartmann-6 benchmark and a new `bayesian_optimization` optional-dependency extra (`tabpfn>=8.1.0`; `botorch` for the example's benchmark objective).
