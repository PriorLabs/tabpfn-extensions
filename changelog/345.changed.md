The shapiq interpretability explainers now route to OddSHAP for first-order Shapley values (`index="SV"`) and ProxySHAP for interaction indices instead of shapiq's `auto` default (KernelSHAP/KernelSHAPIQ); each wrapper gained `approximator` (to override the routing) and `random_state` (for reproducible coalition sampling) arguments. This needs `shapiq>=1.6.0` (Python >= 3.12) plus the `xgboost` and `lightgbm` surrogate backends; on older setups the routing falls back to `auto` with a warning.
